A little bit about us

PK Education Center was started in 2015 by Priya Khemka. The motivation to start this education centre was due to immense love for teaching and to help out children who were very enthusiastic about learning but need proper guidance. This education center is unique that it allows the student to find their strengths among different learning techniques. We provide them with a more customized approach to learning for each individual student.
PK Education has it's primary focus as academics, however, to improve overall cognitive, social skills and keeping well-being of a student's mental health on a high priority, PK Education conducts multi-range spectrum of activies on special occations like Childrens Day or Teacher Day. All students come together, spending quality time with each and being involved in activies that are reacreational, fun and interactive.
