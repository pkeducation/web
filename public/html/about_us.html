A little bit about us ...

In 2007, Priya Khemka,founder and teacher of P.K.Education Centrestarted teaching students as her hobby. After completing CMA in 2017 and realising her passion for teaching, she decided to convert her hobby into her profession and thus, P.K. Education Centre was established. The motivation to start this education centre was immense love for teaching and to help out children who are very enthusiastic about learning but need proper guidance. It is unique as it allows students to find their strengths through/vis-a-vis different learning techniques.
P.K.Education Centre has its primary focus as academics, however, to improve overall cognitive and social skills of students and keeping well-being of a student's mental health on a high priority, it conducts multi-range spectrum of activities on special occasions like Children’s Day or Teachers’ Day. It also conducts various workshops for their skill developments. All students come together, spend quality time with each other and get involved in recreational activities that are fun and interactive.
